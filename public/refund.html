Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Good news: WebClone.io is 100% free, always has been, always will be. No payments, no subscriptions, no hidden fees.

The Short Version

Since we don't charge anything to use WebClone.io, there's nothing to refund. You can clone, edit, and download as many websites as you want — completely free.

Why We're Free

WebClone.io was created as a passion project to help developers, designers, and curious people learn from the web. We believe powerful tools shouldn't require a credit card. Our costs are covered by:

  • 💚 Our own love for building useful things
  • 🛠️ Using free tiers and open-source proxies where possible
  • 🤝 Community support and contributions

What If Something Goes Wrong?

If a website fails to clone, or the AI editor doesn't work as expected, we're sorry — but you haven't lost any money. You can:

  • Try again with a different URL
  • Check our Contact page to report issues
  • Contribute to improving the tool if you're a developer

We do our best to keep the service running smoothly, but as a free tool, there are no service guarantees.

Donations (Not Required, But Appreciated)

If you love WebClone.io and want to support its development, you can:

  • ⭐ Star us on GitHub (if we ever open-source)
  • 🐦 Share the tool on Twitter, LinkedIn, or with friends
  • ☕ Buy us a coffee — contact us for details

No Hidden Costs

We will never surprise you with a bill. There are no premium tiers, no paid features, no "unlock full access" buttons. What you see is what you get — completely free, forever.
