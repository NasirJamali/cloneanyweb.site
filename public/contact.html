Frequently Contacted

Quick answers to common questions

Is WebClone.io really free?
Yes! 100% free, no hidden costs, no premium tiers. Always has been, always will be.
Why won't a website clone?
Some sites have strong bot protection. Try simple sites (example.com, wikipedia.org) first. We're constantly improving proxies.
Can I use cloned sites commercially?
That depends on the original site's license. You're responsible for respecting others' intellectual property.
Do you store the sites I clone?
Never. Everything happens locally in your browser. We don't have servers that store your clones.
