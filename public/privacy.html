Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026 · Version 2.0

Our Commitment to Privacy

At WebClone.io, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our free website cloner and AI editing tools.

Bottom line upfront: We never sell your personal data. All website cloning happens locally in your browser — we don't store the sites you clone.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected (Anonymous)

  • Usage data: Which features you use (cloning, AI editing, downloads) — anonymized and aggregated
  • Error reports: When a fetch fails, we log the error type (not the URL) to improve our proxy engine
  • Referral source: How you found WebClone.io (e.g., Google, Twitter, direct)

Information You Voluntarily Provide

  • Contact form: If you reach out via our Contact page, we collect your name, email, and message to respond
  • Support requests: Any details you share when emailing us directly

What We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ The content of websites you clone (never stored on our servers)
  • ❌ Your IP address (anonymized immediately)
  • ❌ Cookies or tracking identifiers (we use no tracking cookies)
  • ❌ Payment information (we're completely free — no payment needed)

How We Use Your Information

Any data we collect is used only to:

  • Improve the tool's reliability and success rate
  • Respond to your questions and feedback
  • Analyze which proxies work best for different sites
  • Prevent abuse and ensure fair usage

Third-Party Services

WebClone.io uses the following third-party services:

  • Together AI (Qwen3-Coder): When you use the AI editor, the HTML of the page you're editing and your prompt are sent to Together AI's API for processing. We do not store these requests. Together AI Privacy Policy →
  • CORS Proxies: Various free proxies (allorigins.win, corsproxy.io, etc.) are used to fetch websites. These services may see the URL you request, but we have no control over their logging.

Data Retention

We retain anonymous usage logs for up to 30 days for performance analysis. Contact form submissions are kept for up to 6 months. Website clones are never stored — they exist only in your browser until you download them.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold (though we likely hold none)
  • Request deletion of your contact form messages
  • Opt out of any future data collection by simply not using the tool

To exercise these rights, contact us.

Children's Privacy

WebClone.io is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the latest version. Continued use of WebClone.io after changes means you accept the revised policy.
